Frequently asked questions

Do I need my own app store account?+

No — your branded app runs under the OurFarm platform, so you don’t need to manage your own Apple or Google developer account.

How does payment settlement work?+

Payments collected online are reconciled per period into a settlement report showing gross, commission, and net, with payout status tracked until you’re paid.

Can I use my own delivery staff?+

Yes — assign orders to your existing delivery staff and sequence their routes for each run.

Is GST handled automatically?+

Yes — GST is calculated per item automatically as part of the order flow.
